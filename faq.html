1. How could I improve my document quality?

If possible, please change the fonts of documents to be dark and the background to be white when creating document. While scanning a document, you need to properly focus on the camenra so that text will be crisp. Moreover, taking a photo in a bright room without the impact of other lights will help your document more clarity. These tricks only work with text documents, not photos and pictures.

  1. How much time does it take to share document?

Normally, it depends on the number of pages to be share. It usually takes 15 seconds of total

  1. What should I do with failed sharing?

Sometimes, the documents are failed due to some unexpected reasons (i.e., your subscription is expired). You only need to go to home page, click edit and then share again.
